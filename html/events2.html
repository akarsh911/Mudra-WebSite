OUR EVENTS

IZHAAR

One of the biggest fests in Thapar, this event is a fun and enthralling three days fest, where we stage a themed talent battle among the four fierce years of Thapar-University. The cut throat competition keeps everyone on the edge and curiosity fuels in as time nears to the result declaration of the winner of the shining trophy ‘BEST YEAR OF IZHAAR’.

MUDRANITE

Mudra Nite is a much-awaited event where we extend a warm welcome to our talented new batch. It serves as a platform for them to shine, allowing them to showcase their unique talents and potential to their fellow batchmates and respected seniors. It's a night of celebration, fostering connections and creating an encouraging atmosphere where creativity and expression thrive.

SURNITE

Sur Nite is a captivating evening where the stage comes alive with the convergence of melodious voices and enchanting musical instruments. Talented performers showcase their skills, casting a spell with their captivating voices and harmonious tunes. It's a night filled with musical magic, leaving the audience spellbound and immersed in the beauty of soul-stirring melodies.

PLAY-NITE

Play Nite is an enthralling event that brings forth the art of storytelling through beautiful plays and skits. Accomplished actors take the stage, skillfully portraying characters whose emotions resonate deeply within us. With every performance, they touch the very essence of our souls, evoking laughter, tears, and a profound appreciation for the power of theatrical expression.

BRAHMGOSH

Brahmgosh is a sacred gathering where the esteemed presence of our beloved Dean Ma'am graces the occasion. The air resonates with serene Sanskrit chants, creating a soothing ambiance that seeks blessings from the divine for the new year ahead. It is a spiritually uplifting event that fosters a sense of gratitude and positivity among all participants.
