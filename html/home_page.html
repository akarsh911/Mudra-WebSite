 About Us

With every beat of the drum and sway of the dance MUDRA has been the testimony of Thapar's divine culture.It is the canvas where creativity and diversity intertwine to create an enriching and unforgettable experience.

We foster a spirit of inclusivity, embracing the unique stories and perspectives that each individual brings. Through a wide range of events, performances, and workshops, we provide a platform for students to explore their passions, refine their skills, and unleash their artistic potential. At MUDRA we firmly believe that the beauty of humanity lies in its diversity, and we strive to create an inclusive space where every voice is heard and appreciated.

Whether you have a flair for dance, a gift for music, a talent for theater, or a passion for visual arts, our society welcomes you with open arms,we also believe that art has the power to inspire, provoke thought, and unite people.

Amidst the hallowed halls of our institution, a multitude of cultural marvels unfurl, Mudranite ,Surnite and Playnite gives students a sigh of relief from their daily hectic academic schedules.

Followed by our three day flagship event "Izhaar",which is the largest cultural fest at thapar comprising of all the forms of Music Dance and Dramatics.During these three days the entire college feels the aroma of mesmerizing performances and satiating sounds coming right from OAT.

MUDRA in itself isn't just about the performances and events; it's about the friendships and connections that are forged along the way. We are a close-knit community of like-minded individuals who share a deep appreciation for creativity and expression. Together, we support and encourage each other, pushing boundaries and challenging ourselves to reach new heights.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does MUDRA stand for?

Mudra is the acronym for 'Music and Dramatics'.

What is the mission of the society?

Mudra aims at bringing all the talented people of TIET under one umbrella. Mudra is a cutural society which provides all the creative minds a place to socialise and work together.

How can I become a part of Mudra?

Recruitment forms are released each year for new team members. Following which interviews are conducted. The selected candidates are welcomed into the Mudra family.

What skills can I develop by being in the society?

The society has various departments which handle different aspects. Being a part of the society you get to work and learn in these departments. Video editing, content writing, graphic designing are some of the many skills which you can enhance one you come on-board.

Can i participate in the events organised by the society if i am not a part of it?

The society is solely responsible for the management and organisation of the events. Participation is open for all the students of TIET. Auditions are conducted by convenors to finalise participants for different events.

If I become a member of the organising team will I be able to participate in any of the performances during the event?

Yes, you can still participate in performances if you become a member of the organising team. You will have to give auditions like the non-members and if selected, can perform during the event.
